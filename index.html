About Me

Hello, I’m JIANG Zhihan (蒋 之晗). I'm currently a second-year Ph.D. student in the IoT Lab at the University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Dr. Edith C.H. NGAI. I received the bachelor and master degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Xiamen University under the supervision of Dr. Longbiao CHEN.

Research Topics

My research experience spans a range of different topics in Data Analytics & Visualization, Ubiquitous Computing, and Mobile Computing.
