About Me

Hello, I'm Zhihan Jiang, a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at the SEA Lab in the Department of Biomedical Informatics (DBMI) at Columbia University, working with Prof. Xuhai "Orson" Xu. Previously, I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from The University of Hong Kong (Advisor: Prof. Edith C.H. Ngai), and B.E./M.E. in Computer Science and Technology from Xiamen University (Advisor: Prof. Longbiao Chen).

I design and build multimodal AI systems for health sensemaking and actionable intervention. By integrating generative AI, interactive visual analytics, and wearable sensing, I empower individuals and experts to transform heterogeneous health and well-being data into personalized, evidence-based support. My work lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Data Visualization (VIS), and Ubiquitous Computing (UbiComp).

I always welcome the opportunity for research collaboration and I'm eager to explore potential research ideas. If you're interested, please feel free to drop me an email.
