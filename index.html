About Me

Hello, I’m JIANG Zhihan. I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in the IoT Lab at the University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Edith C.H. NGAI. I received the bachelor and master degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Xiamen University under the supervision of Prof. Longbiao CHEN.

My research lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Data Visualization (VIS), Ubiquitous Computing (UbiComp), and Mobile Computing, with a focus on developing mobile analytics systems for empowering pervasive healthcare. By integrating data visualization & analysis and wearable sensing techniques, I aim to foster deeper understanding, effective interaction, and informed decision-making. My work addresses key challenges in Health & Wellness, developing solutions tailored to both individual needs and broader public behaviors, ultimately shaping more responsive, adaptive systems that empower users and communities.

I always welcome the opportunity for research collaboration and I'm eager to explore potential research ideas. If you're interested, please feel free to drop me an email.
