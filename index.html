About Me

Hello, I’m JIANG Zhihan. I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in the IoT Lab at the University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Dr. Edith C.H. NGAI. I received the bachelor and master degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Xiamen University under the supervision of Dr. Longbiao CHEN.


My research interests lie in Data Analytics & Visualization, Ubiquitous Computing, and Mobile Computing.
