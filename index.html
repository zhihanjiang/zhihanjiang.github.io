About Me

Hello, I'm Zhihan JIANG. I will be joining the SEA Lab as a Postdoctoral Research Scientist, working with Prof. Xuhai Orson Xu.

Previously, I received my Ph.D. from the Dept. of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Edith C.H. NGAI. I received the bachelor and master degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Xiamen University, where I was advised by Prof. Longbiao CHEN.

My research lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Data Visualization (VIS), and Ubiquitous Computing (UbiComp), for advancing Health and Well-being. I aim to support deep understanding, effective interaction, and evidence-based decision-making, tailored to both individual needs and broader public behaviors, ultimately shaping more responsive and interpretable interactions that empower individuals and communities.

I always welcome the opportunity for research collaboration and I'm eager to explore potential research ideas. If you're interested, please feel free to drop me an email.
